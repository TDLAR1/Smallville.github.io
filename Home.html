Smallville (TV Series)

Smallville was an American
television series that debuted
October 16, 2001 on the WB
Television Network and ended in
May 13, 2011. Created by Alfred
Gough and Miles Millar, the series
follows the adventures of a young
Clark Kent, in the town of Smallville,
Kansas, before he becomes
Superman.
