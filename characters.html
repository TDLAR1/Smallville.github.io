Clark Kent
BIOGRAPHY

Clark Kent, portrayed by Tom Welling, is the main protagonist of Smallville. A teenager raised in the small town of Smallville, Kansas, Clark discovers that he has extraordinary abilities, including super strength, speed, and the power of flight. As he navigates high school life, Clark grapples with the challenges of keeping his powers a secret while learning about his alien origins. He is known for his strong sense of responsibility, loyalty to his friends and family, and his journey to embrace his destiny as a hero.

Lex Luthor
BIOGRAPHY

Lex Luthor, portrayed by Michael Rosenbaum, is one of the most complex characters in Smallville. Initially introduced as a wealthy and charismatic young man, Lex becomes a key figure in Clark's life. His intelligence and ambition are matched by a growing darkness as he seeks to understand the world around him and his own place in it. Lex’s journey explores themes of power, morality, and the consequences of one’s choices, and his relationship with Clark becomes central to the story as the series progresses.

Lana Lang
BIOGRAPHY

Lana Lang, played by Kristin Kreuk, is one of Clark Kent's closest friends and a key character in Smallville. She is an intelligent and compassionate young woman who struggles with her own personal challenges, including the mystery of her family's past. Lana has a deep connection to Smallville and often acts as a moral compass for the people around her. Throughout the series, she grows emotionally and learns to face the complexities of her relationships and her future.
